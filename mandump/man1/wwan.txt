NAME
       wwan - enable/disable internal wwan (3G/4G) device

SYNOPSIS
       wwan [command]

COMMANDS
       on     Switch device on.

       off    Switch device off.

       toggle Toggle device state.

       <none> Show device state.

       tlp(8).
